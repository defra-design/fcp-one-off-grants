! Warning Do not upload handwritten and scanned copies or PDFs.

Evidence you need to apply

You should check the Water Management grant manual (opens in a new tab) to confirm what evidence you need to provide.

Evidence could include:

  • financial accounts
  • linked businesses and their accounts
  • licences and permissions
  • project funding
  • quotes and tenders
  • VAT in project costs
  • plans and drawings
  • third-party agreements
  • letters of support
