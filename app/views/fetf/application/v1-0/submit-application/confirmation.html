What happens next

The RPA aim to process complete applications within 60 working days.

The RPA will be in touch when they have assessed your full application. They will tell you if your project scored well enough to receive grant funding.

The RPA will assess your application against other projects and value for money. You will not automatically get a grant. The grant is expected to be highly competitive and you are competing against other projects.

If your application is successful, you’ll be sent a grant funding agreement and can begin work on the project.

Withdrawing your application

If you do not want the grant funding, you can withdraw your application before you make a claim.
