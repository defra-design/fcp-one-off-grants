Evidence you need to make a claim


To make a claim, you must provide evidence that each item you are claiming for:

  • has been purchased (for example, invoices)
  • has been paid for in full (for example, receipts and bank statements – you can cross out other transactions)
  • is in place and operational (for example, photos, videos)
