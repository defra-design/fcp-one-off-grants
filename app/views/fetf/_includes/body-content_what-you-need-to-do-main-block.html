Action you need to take to continue with your application.

To continue with your application, you need to buy all the items listed in your grant funding agreement.

Before accepting your grant funding agreement, you should confirm with your supplier that the items are still available.

You must read and accept the agreement by 11:59pm on 31 May 2024 or the offer will expire and we cannot award you a grant.

If you cannot buy all the items

You must tell us as soon as possible if you cannot purchase the item you intended and cannot source it from another supplier or if you cannot meet the claim deadline.

We will consider amendment requests on a case-by-case basis. You must be able to demonstrate you have taken all reasonable steps to source the items within a realistic timeframe.
