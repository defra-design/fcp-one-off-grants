What happens next

The RPA will be in touch by 24 February 2024.

They will tell you if your project scored well enough to get the full application form.

If you submit an application, RPA will assess it against other projects and value for money. You will not automatically get a grant. The grant is expected to be highly competitive and you are competing against other projects.

If your application is successful, you’ll be sent a funding agreement and can begin work on the project.

! You must not start the project

Starting the project or committing to any costs (such as placing orders) before you receive a funding agreement will invalidate your application.

Before you start the project, you can:

  • get quotes from suppliers
  • apply for planning permission or any other licences or permissions required
  • check what evidence you must provide in the Water Management grant manual (opens in a new tab)
  • read How to submit a Water Management grant full application (opens in a new tab)
