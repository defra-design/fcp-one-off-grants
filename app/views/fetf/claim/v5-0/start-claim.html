Claim money for a farming grant

Use this service to claim money for items funded by a farming grant.

What you need to claim

You must have an approved grant funding agreement to claim.

You’ll need to sign in using your Rural Payments service customer reference number and password.

To claim money from your grant you’ll need:

  • item serial numbers
  • invoices for each item
  • bank, building society or credit card statements to show how you paid for the items - these must be from your business account or credit card
  • photos of each item
Claim

Before you claim

Before you claim, check what you need to include in the invoices and bank statements, and how to take item photos.

You cannot use the following to pay for grant-funded items:

  • cash or your personal account
  • lease or hire purchase
  • part exchange
