    If you need to make a change to your project from what is set out in your grant funding agreement, you must get approval from the RPA before you make the change.

  • supplier
  • your business such as status or ownership
  • our grant-funded items such as ownership, usage or any deviation from your agreed project
  • project timescales such as claim submission or completion dates
  • project outputs
  • funding
