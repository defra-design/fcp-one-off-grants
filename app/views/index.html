Farming and Countryside Programme

{{ serviceName }}

The Future Grants service brings together two types of one-off competitive capital grants available to farmers.

Farming Transformation Fund (FTF) provides grants towards large capital items to help businesses improve productivity, profitability and environmental sustainability. These grants are between £25,000 and £500,000.

Farming Equipment and Technology Fund (FETF) provides farmers with grants for equipment or technology. These grants are between £1,000 and £25,000.
