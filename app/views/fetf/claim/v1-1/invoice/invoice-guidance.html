Invoices

We’ll ask you to upload your invoices and tell us which items are on each invoice.

Invoice rules

Invoices must:

  • show the item cost
  • include the manufacturer’s make and model
  • quote the FETF 2024 item code from the item and specification list
  • be addressed to Plumpton Farm Ltd
  • show the supplier’s name, address, VAT number and date
  • be marked by your supplier to show you used a refundable deposit to buy the item, if applicable

If the invoice includes items not in your grant funding agreement, clearly show which items you’re claiming for

Supplier account

If you have a supplier account and you’ve used it to pay more than one invoice, upload copies of all the invoices included in the payment.
