Apply for a grant to improve productivity

Use this service to apply for a Farming Equipment and Technology grant to improve productivity.

Your business must be based and registered in England to apply.

What you’ll need

You’ll need:

  • the names of the items you want to apply for - check the specifications list
  • • financial and business evidence like bank statements and tax returns if you apply for more than £25,000

To use this service, you’ll need an account with the Rural Payments service.

You can apply for someone else if they've given you permission through the Rural Payments service.
