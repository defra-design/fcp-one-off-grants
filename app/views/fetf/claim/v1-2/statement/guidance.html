Bank statements

We’ll ask you to upload a bank, building society or credit card statement and tell us which items are on each statement.

What you can use

The statements must be from a Plumpton Farm Ltd business account or credit card. You can either use:

  • screenshots from an online account showing payment details and bank or building society logos
  • a certified report printed from a banking system

Statement rules

Statements must show the:

  • bank or building society’s name and logo
  • account holder name in full
  • account number
  • transaction date
  • transaction type (including payee reference or cheque number)
  • transaction amount

You can remove personal information that is not needed from the statements.

Invoice amounts

If the transaction amount on the statement does not match the invoice amount, upload either:

  • copies of invoices for the other items you’ve bought
  • a copy of the monthly statement showing the other items you’ve bought, if you have a supplier account

Buying Group

If you pay for an item through a Buying Group, upload an email from the supplier or a supplier statement to show:

  • you’ve paid the Buying Group
  • the Buying Group has paid the supplier
